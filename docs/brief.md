## Executive Summary

AltocV2 is a web platform that revolutionizes CV creation and adaptation through a conversational AI assistant that understands how hiring systems actually work. Unlike traditional editors, AltocV2 combines an integrated chat that edits in real-time, ATS analysis based on real data, intelligent import from LinkedIn and job postings, advanced tone/style personalization, and industry-specialized visual templates.

**The main problem it solves:** (1) friction and time wasted manually adapting CVs, (2) difficulty articulating experience effectively, (3) the "black hole" of not knowing if your CV will pass ATS filters, and (4) the disconnect between LinkedIn profiles, existing CVs, and new applications.

**Key value proposition:**
- **Conversational AI Editor**: Integrated chat that modifies your CV in real-time - you speak naturally, AI edits on visual templates
- **ATS Reality Check**: AI engine trained with real ATS system data that analyzes compatibility and offers one-click fixes
- **Job Match Intelligence**: Automatic analysis of job postings with compatibility score and specific suggestions
- **Smart Import**: Intelligent scraping of LinkedIn, job postings, and existing CVs (PDF) for auto-population
- **Adaptive Writing Assistant**: Tone and style control (formal/casual, technical/accessible) with industry presets
- **Industry-Smart Templates**: Visual templates (not markdown) that understand structure and priorities by sector
- **Effortless Workflow**: Notion-style continuous auto-save, multi-format export, and sharing analytics

**Key differentiator**: The only CV builder where you converse with an AI that truly understands what ATS look for and can read/import from any source.

## Problem Statement

### Current State and Pain Points

**The traditional process of creating and adapting CVs is fragmented, inefficient, and frustrating:**

**For experienced professionals:**
- They have 3-5 versions of their CV in different folders, not knowing which is the most recent
- Adapting a CV for each offer takes 30-60 minutes of manual copy/paste/reformatting
- They don't know if they're using the right keywords that automatic systems look for
- Feedback is non-existent until they (don't) get a response weeks later

**For people with difficulty articulating:**
- They know what they've done, but don't know how to "sell it" professionally
- Generic templates don't help with content, only with format
- Hiring someone to write your CV costs $100-500 USD
- They feel their experience looks "flat" compared to other candidates

**For all users:**
- **ATS black hole**: 75% of CVs never reach human eyes, automatically rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems
- **Data disconnect**: Information already exists on LinkedIn, old CVs, portfolios - but must be manually rewritten
- **Format anxiety**: PDF or Word? One page or two? What font passes filters?
- **Lack of context**: Current tools don't understand the specific offer you're applying to

### Quantifiable Impact

- **Time wasted**: Professionals in active search invest 15-20 hours/month just adapting CVs
- **Opportunity cost**: 60% of qualified candidates are rejected by poorly optimized CVs for ATS, not lack of skills
- **Economic barrier**: Professional CV services are out of reach for 80% of job seekers
- **Emotional stress**: 67% of job seekers report significant anxiety about their CV quality

### Why Existing Solutions Fail

**Traditional editors (Word, Google Docs):**
- Zero intelligence about content
- Inconsistent formatting between versions
- No ATS optimization

**Generic CV Builders (Canva, Resume.io):**
- Pretty templates but generic content
- Don't analyze compatibility with specific jobs
- Rigid and limited editing experience

**AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude):**
- Require complex prompts and multiple iterations
- Not integrated into editing workflow
- No specialized knowledge in ATS
- User has to copy/paste between tools

**LinkedIn:**
- Format not exportable to traditional CV
- No ability to adapt for different roles
- Doesn't analyze compatibility with offers

### Urgency and Importance

**Market timing:**
- The 2025 job market is more competitive than ever (average 200+ applicants per position)
- ATS systems become more sophisticated but less transparent
- Generative AI changed expectations - users know it should be easier
- Remote work expanded global competition - you need to stand out more

**Window of opportunity:**
- Users are adopting AI for work (ChatGPT has 200M+ active users)
- Growing frustration with existing tools that haven't evolved
- Willingness to pay for tools that truly save time

## Proposed Solution

### Core Concept

AltocV2 is a **career conversational assistant** that combines the fluidity of AI chat, the reliability of a professional editor, and the intelligence of specialized recruitment systems. It's not just a CV editor - it's your personal coach that understands both your professional story and the systems that will evaluate your CV.

### Solution Approach

**1. Conversation as Primary Interface**
Instead of facing an empty editor, you start a natural conversation:
- "Help me adapt my CV for this Product Manager offer at Google"
- AI reads the offer (via scraping or paste), analyzes your current CV, and suggests specific changes
- Modifications apply in real-time on the visual editor
- Direct editing always available - chat complements, doesn't replace control

**2. ATS Intelligence Based on Real Data**
AI engine specifically trained with:
- Patterns from real ATS systems (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, etc.)
- Analysis of which CVs pass filters and which don't
- Keywords and formats that maximize compatibility
- Actionable feedback: "Your CV has 67% match. Adding 'Python' in Skills would increase to 85%"

**3. Intelligent Import from Any Source**
Eliminates reinventing the wheel:
- **LinkedIn scraping**: One click to import your complete profile
- **Job posting analysis**: Paste the offer link, AI extracts requirements
- **PDF parsing**: Upload your previous CV, transcribes and structures automatically
- **Smart merge**: Combines information from multiple sources coherently

**4. Intelligent Templates by Industry**
Not just "pretty", but structurally optimized:
- Template "Tech Startup": Prioritizes projects and tech stack over formal education
- Template "Corporate/Finance": Emphasis on quantifiable achievements and progression
- Template "Creative": Balance between portfolio links and experience
- Each template understands which sections matter most in that context

**5. Adaptive Writing Assistant**
Fine control over how you present yourself:
- Personality sliders: Formal ‚Üî Casual, Technical ‚Üî Accessible, Concise ‚Üî Detailed
- Industry presets that adjust entire tone automatically
- Real-time preview of style changes
- AI rewrites maintaining your achievements but adjusting the "voice"

**6. Cloud-First with Auto-Save**
Modern Notion-style experience:
- All your CVs in one cloud workspace
- Continuous auto-save (never lose changes)
- Automatic versioning with timestamps
- Duplicate and adapt in seconds, not minutes

**7. Export & Share Intelligence**
- Multi-format: PDF (ATS-friendly), Word, LinkedIn format, plain text
- Preview of how it looks on different devices
- Sharing links with analytics: did they open it? how long?
- ATS compatibility score before exporting

### Why This Solution Will Succeed

**Perfect technology timing:**
- Current LLMs are good enough for contextual conversation
- Web scraping and PDF parsing are mature technologies
- Cloud infrastructure enables fluid experience

**Combines the best of multiple worlds:**
- Natural conversation of ChatGPT
- Professionalism of CV writing services
- Convenience of visual templates
- Specialized knowledge of recruiters

**Solves the complete problem, not just a part:**
- Other tools help with format OR content OR analysis
- AltocV2 integrates everything into one coherent workflow
- Reduces from 4-5 tools to just one

## MVP Scope

### Core Features (Must Have)

#### **Feature 1: Authentication & User Management with Credit System**
**Description**: Complete user system with integrated credit balance from day 1

**Technical implementation:**
- NextAuth v5 with Google OAuth + Email magic links
- User schema with fields: credits, tier (free/pro), usage tracking
- Middleware to protect routes and verify credits before operations
- Transactional credit deduction logic (avoid race conditions)

**Credit system rules:**
- Free tier: 50 initial credits, 10 credits/month refill
- Pro tier: 500 credits/month + rollover of unused credits
- Costs per operation:
  - Create CV from scratch: 5 credits
  - Adapt existing CV: 3 credits
  - AI chat message: 1 credit
  - ATS analysis: 10 credits
  - Import LinkedIn/PDF: 5 credits
  - Export PDF: 2 credits

**Effort**: Medium (1 week)

---

#### **Feature 2: AI-Powered Conversational Editor**
**Description**: Integrated chat that modifies CV in real-time with multi-model architecture

**Technical implementation:**
- **Chat UI**: Streaming responses (Server-Sent Events or Vercel AI SDK streaming)
- **Model routing strategy**:
  - GPT-4o-mini: General conversation, simple suggestions (cheap, fast)
  - GPT-4o: Complex rewriting, deep analysis (expensive, better quality)
  - Claude 3.5 Haiku: Formatting, structure (economical alternative)
- **Context management**:
  - Prompt system with CV current state in context
  - Limited history window (last 10 messages for cost control)
- **Action execution**:
  - AI generates "actions" in structured format (JSON)
  - Frontend applies actions to CV state optimistically
  - Rollback if operation fails

**Effort**: High (2 weeks)

---

#### **Feature 3: Fine-Tuned ATS Analyzer**
**Description**: Custom model trained to analyze compatibility with ATS systems

**Technical implementation:**

**Phase 1 - Training data collection:**
- Scrape 500-1000 job postings from Indeed/LinkedIn
- Use public examples of "CVs that passed ATS"
- Synthesize examples with GPT-4
- Dataset format: `{ job_description, cv_text, compatibility_score, issues[], suggestions[] }`

**Phase 2 - Fine-tuning:**
- Fine-tune GPT-4o-mini or Claude Haiku on dataset
- Alternative: Use GPT-4o with few-shot examples if fine-tuning is too expensive/slow
- Objective: Model that identifies specific issues (formatting, missing keywords, structure)

**Phase 3 - Analysis pipeline:**
- Parse CV structure
- Extract keywords from job (if provided)
- Run fine-tuned model
- Return actionable feedback with one-click fixes

**Effort**: Very High (2-3 weeks)

---

#### **Feature 4: Smart Import System**
**Description**: Robust parsing of LinkedIn profiles, PDFs, and job postings

**Technical implementation:**

**LinkedIn Import (Phase 2 - post-MVP):**
- User exports LinkedIn PDF ‚Üí parse with pdf-parse + GPT-4 vision
- Extract: Name, headline, experience, education, skills
- Normalize dates, company names, formatting

**PDF CV Import:**
- pdf-parse for plain text
- GPT-4 Vision for complex/multi-column PDFs
- Prompt: "Extract CV sections: contact info, experience, education, skills, etc."
- Output: Structured JSON that populates the editor

**Job Posting Parser:**
- User pastes URL or text of offer
- Extract: Required skills/keywords, job title, company, responsibilities
- Feed this to AI chat for context

**Effort**: High (1.5 weeks)

---

#### **Feature 5: Visual CV Editor with Templates**
**Description**: WYSIWYG editor with professional templates exportable to PDF

**Technical implementation:**

**Editor state management:**
- Zustand store for CV data structure
- Sections: personal, summary, experience, education, skills, projects

**Templates (start with 3):**
1. **Modern Tech**: Single column, bold headers, tech stack highlighted
2. **Traditional Corporate**: Two column, conservative styling
3. **Creative**: More visual, color accents, portfolio links prominent

**Editing UX:**
- Click-to-edit inline
- Drag-to-reorder sections
- Auto-save every 3 seconds (debounced)
- Undo/redo stack

**PDF Export:**
- @react-pdf/renderer for rendering
- Generate on backend (serverless function)
- Return signed URL of PDF stored in Vercel Blob

**Effort**: High (2 weeks)

---

#### **Feature 6: Stripe Payment Integration**
**Description**: Complete payment system with webhooks and subscription management

**Pricing tiers:**
- **Free**: $0/month - 50 initial credits, 10/month refill, 3 CVs max
- **Pro**: $9.99/month - 500 credits/month, unlimited CVs, all templates
- **Credits Pack**: $4.99 for 100 additional credits (one-time purchase)

**Stripe setup:**
- Stripe Checkout for subscriptions
- Stripe Customer Portal for subscription management
- Webhooks for payment events
- Idempotency handling

**Effort**: Medium (1 week)

---

#### **Feature 7: Job Matching with Embeddings**
**Description**: Vector similarity between CV and job offers for scoring

**Technical implementation:**
- Use OpenAI `text-embedding-3-small`
- Embed CV sections separately
- Embed job description
- Calculate cosine similarity
- Extract missing keywords via LLM
- Return match score + suggestions

**Effort**: Medium (1 week)

## Technical Considerations

### Technology Stack

**Frontend:**
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui
- **State Management**: Zustand + TanStack Query
- **Type Safety**: TypeScript (strict mode) + Zod

**Backend:**
- **API**: Next.js Server Actions + API Routes (no tRPC initially)
- **Auth**: NextAuth.js v5
- **File Storage**: Vercel Blob Storage
- **Background Jobs**: Trigger.dev or Inngest

**Database:**
- **Primary DB**: PostgreSQL via Neon (Serverless)
- **ORM**: Prisma
- **Caching**: Upstash Redis
- **Vector DB**: Supabase pgvector (start simple)

**AI/ML Stack:**
- **Primary LLM**: OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini)
- **Secondary LLM**: Anthropic (Claude 3.5 Haiku, Sonnet)
- **Embeddings**: OpenAI text-embedding-3-small
- **Fine-tuning**: GPT-4o-mini fine-tuning API

**Infrastructure:**
- **Host**: Vercel
- **Email**: Resend
- **Monitoring**: Sentry (errors) + PostHog (analytics)
- **Payments**: Stripe
